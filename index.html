ABOUT ANURAGIT SOLUTIONS



Anurag IT Solutions is a leading provider of innovative technology services and training solutions. Our mission is to empower students, graduates, and professionals with industry-relevant skills and hands-on experience through internship programs, software training, and real-world project exposure.


Program Details

The training Program is tailored for engineering graduates in Computer Science and IT disciplines, offering students three internship options at ANURAG IT SOLUTIONS Rajahmundry.

STUDENT CAN CHOOSE ANY ONE MODE FOR THE INTERNSHIP

1
ONLINE TRAINING

Students will engage in a self-paced online training program with recorded video sessions, supported by weekly faculty-led doubt-clearing sessions for guidance and clarity.
2
HYBRID TRAINING

The hybrid training program combines 70% online learning with 30% hands-on training at ANURAG IT SOLUTIONS Rajahmundry, with limited seats on a first-come, first-serve basis.
3
OFFLINE TRAINING

The offline training program offers 100% hands-on experience at ANURAG IT SOLUTIONS Rajahmundry, with placement assistance, limited seats on a first-come, first-serve basis.
